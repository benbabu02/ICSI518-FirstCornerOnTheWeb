About me

Hey! My name is Benjamin, I'm am currently pursing a Master's in Computer Science from the University at Albany. I have worked on a wide range of projects, simulating a computer processor in Java to creating interactive games and tools with JavaScript and Python.

At UAlbany, I served as the Manager of IEEE Computer Society, where I helped grow the organization through events, hackathons, and hands-on workshops. I focused on promoting computer science on campus and creating opportunities for students to explore tech, collaborate and build real-world skills.

I enjoy solving problems, learning new frameworks, and making tech more creative and accessible. I am also into photography! I love capturing beautiful and interesting places through a lens.

Feel free to checkout my GitHub or my photography page
